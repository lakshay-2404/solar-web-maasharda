Bahut saare customers ko lagta hai ki DCR kisi brand ka naam hai. Reality mein **DCR ek government compliance rule hai, brand nahi**.

DCR ka full form hai **Domestic Content Requirement**. Subsidy-linked rooftop projects mein iska simple matlab hota hai ki approved Indian manufacturing framework ke hisaab se panel choose kiye jayein.

Seedhi baat: subsidy chahiye to panel selection random nahi ho sakta.

## Cells aur modules dono ka role

Customer ko factory sourcing detail samajhne ki zarurat nahi hoti, lekin installer ko clear hona chahiye ki **cells aur modules dono Made in India alignment** ke saath aayein.

Isi wajah se DCR panel ka quote kabhi-kabhi thoda higher lag sakta hai, lekin compliance safe rehti hai.

## ALMM kya hota hai?

ALMM ka full form hai **Approved List of Models and Manufacturers**. Simple language mein, approved panel models aur approved manufacturers ki list ko dekhkar compliant product choose kiya jaata hai.

Customer ko ALMM list khud study karne ki zarurat nahi. Installer ka kaam hai aapko compliant shortlist dena.

## Subsidy ke liye DCR mandatory kyun?

Government scheme domestic manufacturing ecosystem ko support karti hai. Isi liye subsidy-linked project mein DCR requirement critical hoti hai.

Practical result: **non-DCR lagaya to subsidy nahi milegi**. Aur iska direct matlab ho sakta hai **Rs 78,000 tak ka loss**.

## DCR vs non-DCR comparison

| Parameter | DCR | Non-DCR |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Subsidy eligibility | Yes | No |
| Typical pricing | 10%-15% higher ho sakti hai | Slightly cheaper |
| Compliance safety | High | Risky |
| Net practical value | Strong for subsidy case | Cheap quote, weak outcome |

Yahan biggest question yeh hota hai ki **net benefit kis option mein zyada hai**. Sasti quote dekhna easy hota hai, lekin subsidy miss hone par poora equation ulta ho jaata hai.

## Kaunse brands DCR options dete hain?

Market mein Luminous, Adani, UTL, SunEnergy aur dusre approved names frequently sunne ko milte hain. Lekin sirf brand naam dekhna enough nahi hota; exact approved product line matter karti hai.
