Solar lene ka decision sirf brand ya kW size ka decision nahi hota. Pehla bada question hota hai: **system type kaunsa hoga?**

Most customers teen options sunte hain: on-grid, off-grid aur hybrid. In teenon ka role alag hai, isliye galat type choose karne se budget bhi hurt hota hai aur daily experience bhi.

## On-grid kya hota hai?

On-grid system **grid se connected** hota hai aur usually **battery nahi rakhta**. Yeh sabse affordable option hota hai aur subsidy-oriented homes mein sabse common bhi.

Iski biggest limitation yeh hai ki **grid outage ke time supply available nahi hoti**. Yani bill savings ke liye great, lekin backup ke liye nahi.

## Off-grid kya hota hai?

Off-grid system **grid se connected nahi hota**. Battery mandatory hoti hai aur poora design independent operation ke liye plan hota hai.

Remote ya special use-case mein yeh useful ho sakta hai, lekin normal city aur town homes ke liye yeh usually sabse expensive category hoti hai.

## Hybrid kya hota hai?

Hybrid system **grid + battery dono** ko combine karta hai. Normal days mein bill savings milti hai aur outage ke waqt backup support bhi.

Yeh un homes ke liye strong fit hota hai jahan power cuts real problem hain aur customer alag inverter-battery jugaad nahi chahte.

## Detailed comparison table

| Parameter | On-grid | Off-grid | Hybrid |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Grid se connection | Yes | No | Yes |
| Battery | No | Yes | Yes |
| Subsidy | Yes | Usually no | Yes, compliant cases mein |
| Power cut mein supply | No | Yes | Yes |
| Typical cost | Lowest | High | Premium |

Short version:

- On-grid: cheapest
- Off-grid: independent
- Hybrid: balanced and backup-ready

## Practical recommendation

Most urban aur semi-urban homes ke liye **on-grid ya hybrid** hi real options hote hain. Off-grid tabhi consider karna chahiye jab location ya use-case truly independent setup demand karta ho.

North Haryana belt ke kaafi homes mein grid available hoti hai, lekin cuts aur fluctuation ka pattern location-wise alag ho sakta hai. Isi liye final recommendation bill, rooftop aur backup need dekhkar hi deni chahiye.
