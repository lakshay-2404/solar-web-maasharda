Hybrid solar system un homes ke liye useful option hai jo **bill savings** aur **power cut backup** dono chahte hain. Simple definition yeh hai ki hybrid system grid se connected bhi hota hai aur battery backup bhi rakhta hai.

On-grid sirf bill reduction deta hai, off-grid full independence deta hai, lekin hybrid in dono ka balanced version hota hai. Isi liye mixed urban aur semi-urban areas mein hybrid ka relevance strong hota hai.

## Subsidy hybrid par milti hai kya?

Haan, practical residential planning mein **hybrid system subsidy-eligible ho sakta hai**, provided configuration compliant ho. Subsidy logic generally on-grid slabs ke hi framework par samjha ja sakta hai.

Yeh yaad rakho ki **3kW ke baad subsidy nahi badhti**. Hybrid lene ka main reason extra subsidy nahi, balki backup capability hota hai.

## Power cut ke waqt kya hota hai?

Jab grid chali jaati hai, hybrid inverter battery ko use karke selected circuits ko backup deta hai. Iska matlab poora ghar unlimited time tak nahi chalega, lekin important load smooth chal sakta hai.

Common backup loads:

- lights
- fans
- Wi-Fi
- fridge
- TV
- selected comfort loads

## On-grid vs off-grid vs hybrid

| Type | Grid | Battery | Subsidy | Outage mein power | Typical cost |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| On-grid | Yes | No | Yes | No | ~Rs 45,000/kW se shuru |
| Off-grid | No | Yes | No | Yes | ~Rs 60,000-Rs 70,000/kW |
| Hybrid | Yes | Yes | Yes | Yes | ~Rs 65,000-Rs 80,000/kW |

Table se clear hai ki hybrid sabse flexible hai, lekin sabse budget-friendly nahi. Isi liye hybrid tab recommend hota hai jab backup need genuinely ho.

## Cost difference kyon hota hai?

On-grid simple architecture hota hai, isliye cost lower rehti hai. Hybrid mein battery bank, hybrid inverter, protection logic aur backup design add hota hai, isliye per-kW pricing higher hoti hai.

Frequent power cuts wale homes mein hybrid ka extra budget practical comfort deta hai. Jahan cuts rare hain, wahan on-grid bhi kaafi hota hai.

## Battery sizing kaise decide hoti hai?

Battery ka answer har customer ke liye same nahi hota. Yeh depend karta hai ki aapko kitne ghante backup chahiye aur kaunse load chalane hain.

Basic backup ke liye smaller battery enough ho sakti hai. Heavy load ya longer support ke liye bigger battery planning chahiye hoti hai.
