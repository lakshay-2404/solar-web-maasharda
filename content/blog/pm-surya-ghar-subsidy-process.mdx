## Eligibility criteria

Basic eligibility ko simple form mein samjho:

1. Domestic electricity connection hona chahiye.
2. Rooftop usable aur structurally suitable hona chahiye.
3. Applicant ke identity aur address documents valid hone chahiye.
4. Bank details aur applicant details properly match honi chahiye.
5. Installed system ko DCR-compliant framework follow karna chahiye.

In points mein weakness ho to process delay ya rejection dono ho sakte hain. Isi liye pre-check bahut zaruri hota hai.

## Subsidy amount kitni hoti hai?

Residential rooftop planning mein common slabs:

- **1kW = Rs 30,000**
- **2kW = Rs 60,000**
- **3kW and above = Rs 78,000 max cap**

Yahi wajah hai ki 3kW planning bahut popular hoti hai. 3kW ke baad subsidy amount nahi badhti, isliye system sizing actual need ke hisaab se hi karni chahiye.

## DCR mandatory kyun hai?

DCR ka matlab hai **Domestic Content Requirement**. Subsidy-linked project mein panel selection sirf price par based nahi ho sakta.

Practical rule simple hai: **galat panel choose kiya to subsidy atak sakti hai**. Isliye project ki compliance shuruaat se hi sahi honi chahiye.

## Complete process: practical 8 steps

1. Bill review aur rooftop requirement discussion  
2. Site survey aur usable area assessment  
3. System sizing aur compliant material planning  
4. Documents collection  
5. Load passing aur file preparation  
6. Portal filing aur approval follow-up  
7. Installation aur inspection coordination  
8. Meter change aur subsidy completion tracking

Yeh wahi point hai jahan Maa Sharda ka process alag hota hai. Hum sirf panel supply nahi karte, balki poore workflow ka ownership lete hain.

## Kaunse documents ready rakhne chahiye?

Typical list:

- Aadhaar
- Latest electricity bill
- Bank passbook ya bank details
- Property proof

Kabhi-kabhi supporting declaration ya extra document bhi lag sakta hai. Isliye hum pehle hi checklist clear kar dete hain.

## Timeline aur common delays

Installation aur submission ke baad subsidy credit ko practical planning mein **30 se 60 din** ka window maan kar chalna chahiye. Delay usually wrong document, mismatched bank detail, incomplete file ya non-compliant material ki wajah se hota hai.

Humara objective yahi hota hai ki ye mistakes start se hi avoid ho jayein.
