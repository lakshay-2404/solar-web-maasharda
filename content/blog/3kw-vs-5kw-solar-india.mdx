3kW aur 5kW dono popular sizes hain, lekin dono har ghar ke liye same tarah useful nahi hote. Sahi choice monthly bill, rooftop space, daytime load aur future expansion ke hisaab se decide hoti hai.

Sabse important fact yeh hai ki **subsidy 3kW ke baad nahi badhti**. Yani 3kW ko bhi maximum cap mil sakti hai aur 5kW ko bhi wahi cap milti hai.

Isi ek point ki wajah se 3kW vs 5kW comparison sirf output ka nahi, **budget aur value** ka comparison ban jaata hai.

## 3kW system ka output

3kW system se practical planning mein **around 400-450 units per month** generation maana ja sakta hai. Humari calculator baseline ke hisaab se yeh **around 420 units per month** ke aas-paas baithta hai.

Rs 8 per unit rate par monthly savings **around Rs 3,360** ban sakti hain. Yeh range un homes ke liye strong hoti hai jinka monthly bill roughly **Rs 2,000 se Rs 5,000** ke beech hota hai.

Subsidy-compliant DCR 3kW planning mein net budget ko roughly **Rs 1,00,000** ke aas-paas dekhna zyada practical hai.

## 5kW system ka output

5kW system se practical estimate **around 700 units per month** generation ka banta hai. Same electricity rate par monthly savings **around Rs 5,600** ke aas-paas aa sakti hain.

Lekin subsidy yahan bhi **Rs 78,000 cap** par hi rukti hai. Iska matlab extra 2kW ka additional hardware aur installation cost customer side par hi rehta hai.

Yeh size un customers ke liye better hota hai jinka bill **Rs 5,000 se Rs 10,000** ho, ya jahan daytime load genuinely heavy ho.

## Practical comparison table

| Parameter | 3kW | 5kW |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Monthly generation | ~420 units | ~700 units |
| Monthly savings | ~Rs 3,360 | ~Rs 5,600 |
| Annual savings | ~Rs 40,320 | ~Rs 67,200 |
| Subsidy | Rs 78,000 max | Rs 78,000 same cap |
| Approx net DCR budget | ~Rs 1,00,000 | ~Rs 2.18 lakh |
| Best fit | Rs 2,000-Rs 5,000 bill | Rs 5,000-Rs 10,000 bill |

Table se insight clear hai: **subsidy same rehti hai, lekin 5kW ka extra 2kW cost fully customer bear karta hai**. Isliye 5kW tabhi lena chahiye jab actual load usse justify kare.

## Kaun 3kW le?

3kW un homes ke liye accha hai jahan rooftop moderate ho, bill controlled range mein ho aur customer ko subsidy-backed value chahiye. Yeh size bahut households ko practical savings deta hai.

## Kaun 5kW le?

5kW tab better hota hai jab multiple ACs, higher daytime use, future floor addition, office load ya small commercial pattern ho. Agar current bill already high hai to 5kW zyada practical answer ho sakta hai.

## Final decision

3kW subsidy value aur manageable budget ke liye strong choice hai. 5kW higher output ke liye strong choice hai. Dono mein se sahi वही hai jo aapke actual bill aur usage pattern se match kare.
