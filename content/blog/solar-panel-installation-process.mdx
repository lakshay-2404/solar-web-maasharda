Bahut log solar lena chahte hain, lekin unhe exact process clear nahi hota. Real concern hota hai: kitni daud-dhoop hogi, kitna time lagega, aur customer ko khud kya karna padega?

Ideal professional setup mein customer ko bahut kam kaam karna padta hai. **Aapko bas documents dene hote hain, confirmations complete karni hoti hain aur installation day par available rehna hota hai.**

## Step 1: Free site survey

Maa Sharda team rooftop inspect karti hai. Hum shadow, direction, usable area, structure condition aur cable route check karte hain.

## Step 2: System design aur proposal

Survey ke baad recommended kW size, estimated generation aur practical budget ke saath proposal banaya jaata hai. Yahin par on-grid ya hybrid jaise decisions clear hote hain.

## Step 3: DCR panel aur inverter planning

Subsidy-linked residential project mein compliant material selection bahut important hota hai. Is stage par DCR panel, inverter aur zarurat ho to battery planning final hoti hai.

## Step 4: Documents collection aur file preparation

Customer se required documents lekar hum subsidy file, load passing aur approval-related paperwork ready karte hain. Yahan se actual backend process start hota hai.

## Step 5: Portal filing aur approval follow-up

Portal filing aur UHBVN coordination humari side se hota hai. Isi phase mein customer ka paperwork structured flow mein aage badhta hai.

## Step 6: Installation

Physical installation usually **1-2 din** mein complete ho sakti hai, depending on system size aur site complexity. Mounting, panels, inverter, earthing aur wiring sab structured tareeke se hota hai.

## Step 7: Inspection aur meter change

Installation ke baad inspection aur meter change process coordinated hota hai. Ye wahi step hai jahan most homeowners khud atak jaate hain, isliye installer ownership bahut valuable hoti hai.

## Step 8: Final submission aur subsidy tracking

Approvals aur final process ke baad subsidy completion track ki jaati hai. Practical expectation **30-60 days** ka rakhna chahiye.

## Customer ko kya karna hota hai?

Almost nothing. Access available rakhna hota hai, documents dene hote hain aur final confirmations complete karni hoti hain. Isi liye hum kehte hain: **aapko bas ghar pe rehna hai, baaki hum karte hain**.

## Maintenance mein kya chahiye?

Normal rooftop solar ko heavy maintenance nahi chahiye hoti. Basic cleaning, occasional visual check aur simple monitoring enough hota hai.
